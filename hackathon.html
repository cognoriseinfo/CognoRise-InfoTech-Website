Web Dev Hackathon

We thank you for showing interest in participating in the 2024 CognoRise InfoTech Web Development Hackathon - September edition. This consists of only 1 Round. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, this hackathon is a great opportunity to build something cool, meet like-minded individuals, and have fun!

Registration

The participants are requested to fill the form,the form will let us know if you are interested in participating in our hackathon. After you fill the form, we will send you an email where you will receive further instructions. Please go through all the rules before you fill the form

Rules

  • For this hackathon, you have to build a Restaurant inventory website.
  • You can use any technology stack to build the landing page
  • Source code submitted will be checked for the plagiarism and copied submissions will not be considered.
  • We will roll out the submission form later.
  • This is an Individual Event.
  • The submission date is from 20/09/2024 - 28/09/2024
  • We will declare the results on 30/09/2024.
  • All the participants will receive certificates, and the winners will get cash prizes worth upto Rs 4000 !!

Eligibility Criteria

  • People who are pursuing their degree in college or passed out can appear in this hackathon.
  • You need to have a whatsapp number and a proper email for communication.
  • You need to pay an Entry Fee of 50 INR to participate in this hackathon.
We hope you have fun participating in the hackathon!! All the very best!!
