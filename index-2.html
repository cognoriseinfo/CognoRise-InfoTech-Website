Web Dev Hackathon

We thank you for showing interest in participating in the 2024 CognoRise InfoTech Web Dev Hackathon- March edition. This consists of 2 Rounds. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, this hackathon is a great opportunity to build something cool, meet like-minded individuals, and have fun!

Registration

The participants are requested to fill the form,the form will let us know if you are interested in participating in our hackathon. After you fill the form, we will send you an email where you will receive further instructions. Please go through all the rules before you fill the form

Rules

  • For Round 1 this hackathon, you have to build a Landing page for an educational website.
  • You can use any technology stack to build the landing page.
  • We will roll out the submission form later.
  • This is an Individual Event.
  • The submission date for Round 1 is from 15/03/2024 - 18/03/2024.
  • We will declare the results of the people who got selected for Round 2 on 20/03/2024.
  • The instructions about Round 2 will be shared later!.
  • All the participants will receive certificates, and the winners will get cash prizes!.

Eligibility Criteria

  • People who are pursuing their degree in college or passed out can appear in this hackathon.
  • You need to have a whatsapp number and a proper email for communication.
  • You need to pay an Entry Fee of 50rs INR to participate in this hackathon.
We hope you have fun participating in the hackathon!! All the very best!!

~ CognoRise InfoTech Team
